Government Programs
---------------------

Medicare - Funded and Administered by Federal Government

Eligible
--------
Age 65 and above
Disabled
Rail Road Retiree
ESRD

Medicare Part A
---------------
Covers hospital benefits
Traditional Idemnity Plan
Provider Paid FFS
No premium (Govt sponsored/free)
Deductible
Coinsurance

Medicare Part B
---------------
Covers Non Hospital Benefits (Physician/Outpatient)
Idemnity Plan
Member need to pay Premium (differs based on member's income)
Deductible
Coinsurance
Missed enrollment will have penalty

Medicare Part A + Medicare Part B ==> Original Medicare Plan

Medicare Part C (Medicare Advantage Plan/MA Plan)
-------------------------------------------------

Part A + Part B + Extra Benefits

Extra Benefits mostly Pharmacy Benefits, sometimes HMO, PPO

Part D (PDP/ Prescription Drug Plan )
-----------------------------------------
Covers Pharmacy Benefits
Member pays Premium (but the premium will be less)
Late enroll - Penalties


Medigap (Medicare Supplemental Plan)
------------------------------------
Extra insurance you can buy to help pay the costs the Original Medicare doesn't fully cover

Medicaid (low income population)
---------
Funded by Federal Govt and Administered by State GOvt

Eligible
--------
Children
Pregnant Women
Disabled
Adult and Elderly

Disabled and Elderly are eligible for both Medicare and Medicaid --> Dual Eligible Members

Medicare - 1st to cover the expenses
Medicaid - 2nd to cover the expenses

CMS - Centre for Medicare and Medicaid Services
